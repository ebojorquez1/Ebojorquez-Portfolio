Designer's Bio

My journey with web design began in the summer of 2021 at Texas State Technical College. In less than a year I have learned more than I ever would have imagined. I have gained a strong foundation in Web Design & Development. This along with my aptitude of learning new things quickly, has confidently prepared me to make it a profession.

What began as an opportunity to gain knowledge in web design and development through a certification program led me through a path that chanels my creativity and challenges my strength of data and analytical decision making.I value the commitment of teamwork, trust and above all - strong work ethic. As a former educator, I believe in being a lifelong learner. That being said, I have undoubtedly found my niche!
