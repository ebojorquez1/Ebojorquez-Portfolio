Favorite TV Shows:

Dexter
Showtime aired this original series from 2006-2013. Dexter is an introverted, hard working, and dependable blood splatter expert who also happens to be a serial killer. The series gives its viewers flashbacks of Dexters childhood allowing a sense of understanding and compassion as to why he does what he does...kill.
Entourage
This HBO original series aired from 2004-2011. Vince Chase, a young rising star from Queens, and his entourage are enjoying the perks of their new found fame. This comedy includes passion, pitfalls and extreme indulgance. In 2015, four years after the season finale, Entourage was released as a major motion picture and gave fans a final glimpse of Vinnie and the boys.
Homeland
A Showtime Original Series that aired from 2011-2020. Carrie is a bipolar CIA agent that has dedicated her life to her job and often finds herself overly involved with little to no boundaries. Such is the case as she crosses the line and becomes romantically involved with a Marine POW who has been turned by al-Qaeda. Viewers will share in the thrill, mystery and romance that this series delivers.
Beverly Hills 90210
FOX aired this series between 1990-2000 and its popularity helped put the new station on the map as a top television network. The 90210 gang offers a larger than life look at beverly hills high school teens. Don't let the glamour fool you, these teens share their battles with addiction, suicide, rape, teen pregnancy and much more.
Ted Lasso
This Apple TV+ Original Series first aired in 2020 and is currently in its second season. It follows an sweet, high spirited and corny American football coach who agrees to fly accross the world and coach and struggling soccer team in the U.K. Did I mention he has NEVER coached soccer before?
