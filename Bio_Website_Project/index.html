About Me

I'm a Texas Girl, born and raised. I was born on March 13, 1978, in Brownsville, Texas, and am your typical cliche Pieces. I am creative, outgoing, caring, and strong willed (although my husband prefers to use the word stubborn). I was a teacher and administrator for almost 20 years until the summer of 2021 when I made the decission to focus on my family and persue my goal of becoming an independent consultant. Although I am no longer working full time, I am far from "not working". A few things my kiddos are currently keeping me busy with include a college and high school graduation as well as my little one's first year of preschool (bus pickup and all)!

My life beyond my children includes gardening (which none of my children share and I'm okay with that), cooking, and letting my husband spoil me with back massages, wine and a cheesey lifetime movie. I LOVE expressing my analytical thinking and creativity to help others take their business to levels they never thought possible before. I'm driven by the possibilities of growth that my hard work and experience can provide.
