I work hard in a field I love and keep busy with a family of seven, but whenever possible I try to incorporate the hobbies and interests I enjoy doing with my family. Although the teenagers no longer find in cool to cook with mom, I can still rely on Olivia (4yr) being mommy's assistand in the kitchen. The same could be said for my time spent gardening or working on home improvements. If I'm being totally honest, my older children have shared that they rather keep their distance when mom is working on her "projects". I don't complain since my "projects" equally serve as my R&R time. One hobby that has seemed to link everyone's interest is puzzlemaking. And be warned - THE PUZZLE MUST BE COMPLETED BEFORE WE SLEEP - or it mysteriously ends up completed by morning. And when it comes to getting away...Vegas Baby!

Baking traditional foods and exploring new recipes is a staple in our family.

Although I may not get enough time to work on my garden as I would like, it is always one of my favorite things to do.

Whether it's painting a craft with my children, on a canvas or trying out a new look on our walls, painting is one of my most satisfying hobbies.

Rest and Relaxation is highly valued in my book!

Puzzles are a great way for me (with or without my family) to unwind while my mind continues working to complete the task at hand.

When it comes to escaping for a quick getaway, you are sure to find my husband and I playing at a nearby casino if available.
